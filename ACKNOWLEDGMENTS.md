## Adaptations for Prosperity 4

This Prosperity 4 version includes the following modifications:
- Updated package structure from `prosperity3bt` to `prosperity4bt`
- New product definitions: EMERALDS (limit: 80), TOMATOES (limit: 80)
- Removed Prosperity 3 products and their position limits
- Updated environment variables: `PROSPERITY4BT_ROUND`, `PROSPERITY4BT_DAY`
- Simplified CLI (removed visualizer integration)
- Updated documentation for Prosperity 4 specifics
